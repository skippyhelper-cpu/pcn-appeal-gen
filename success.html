What Happens Next?

Will the 50% discount be frozen? expand_more

Yes, in most cases. If you appeal within the first 14 days, the council will usually freeze the discount rate until they make a decision.

What if my appeal is rejected? expand_more

If the council rejects your informal appeal, they will send a "Notice of Rejection." You can then choose to pay at the discount rate or take it to an independent tribunal.

Should I pay the fine now? expand_more

No. Paying the fine is an admission of liability, which usually closes the case and prevents you from appealing further.
