Formal Challenge

Date: October 24, 2023

Parking Services

PO Box 352

London, E15 2QR

Dear Parking Appeals Department,

RE: Formal representation against PCN No: AB12345678

I am writing to formally challenge the above-referenced Penalty Charge Notice (PCN), which was issued on 22/10/2023 at 14:15 for the alleged contravention of Code 01 (Parked in a restricted street during prescribed hours).

My challenge is based on the following grounds: The alleged contravention did not occur due to inadequate and non-compliant signage.

According to The Local Authorities' Traffic Orders (Procedure) (England and Wales) Regulations 1996, Regulation 18 requires the traffic authority to place signs that provide adequate information as to the effect of an order. In this specific instance, the time plate governing the restricted zone was obscured by overgrown foliage and was not visible from the position where my vehicle was parked.

Furthermore, I wish to highlight a procedural impropriety. The PCN was issued less than two minutes after the observation period began. This fails to acknowledge the necessary 'grace period' established by the Secretary of State's Statutory Guidance, which suggests a minimum of ten minutes for restricted street contraventions where a vehicle was parked legally just before the restriction changed.

I have attached photographic evidence clearly showing the obscured sign and the position of my vehicle. Given these circumstances, I trust that this PCN will be cancelled immediately.

If you choose to reject this representation, please provide a detailed explanation as to why the signage is considered adequate despite the photographic evidence provided, and issue a Notice of Rejection along with details of how to appeal to the independent adjudicator.

Yours faithfully,

[Electronic Signature Applied]

J. Doe
