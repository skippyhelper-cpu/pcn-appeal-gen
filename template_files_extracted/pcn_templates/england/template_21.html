Having reviewed the circumstances and the evidence available, I believe the Penalty Charge Notice should be cancelled on the following grounds.

1. The Contravention Did Not Occur — The Bay Was Not Lawfully Suspended

{{CIRCUMSTANCES}}

A bay suspension must be implemented in accordance with the authority's powers and the relevant TRO or, in the case of temporary suspensions, by means of a proper suspension notice lawfully placed and with adequate advance notice. TPT adjudicators have consistently held that where the suspension notice was not properly affixed, was illegible, or was placed with insufficient notice, the PCN cannot be sustained: see, for example, Lambeth LBC v Appellant (TPT, various).

2. Inadequate Notice of Suspension

The authority must give advance notice of a bay suspension, generally by affixing suspension notices to the posts of the parking bay signs at least 24 hours before the suspension takes effect (longer where practicable). Where this requirement was not met, parking in the bay does not constitute a contravention, as the motorist had no reasonable means of knowing the bay was suspended.

3. Suspension Sign Compliance

Suspension notices must comply with the relevant statutory requirements and must be clearly legible. Any sign that was damaged, obscured or incorrectly positioned would undermine the lawfulness of the suspension. I request copies of the suspension notice(s) and evidence of when and how they were placed.

4. Procedural Impropriety

The PCN must comply with Section 66 of the Traffic Management Act 2004 and the Civil Enforcement of Parking Contraventions (England) General Regulations 2007. Where the suspension itself was not lawfully implemented, issuing a PCN for code 21 constitutes a procedural impropriety.

6. Legal Framework

Bay suspensions may be implemented under Section 15 of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984 or under the relevant TRO. The authority must demonstrate that the suspension was lawfully implemented, that proper notice was given, and that the suspension notice was compliant and legible at the time of parking.

In light of the above, I respectfully request that the Penalty Charge Notice is cancelled in full.
