1. The Contravention Did Not Occur

{{CIRCUMSTANCES}}

Code 31 is a moving traffic contravention. The contravention occurs only where a vehicle enters the yellow box when the exit is not clear. Where the exit was clear at the moment of entry but subsequently became blocked due to other road users, no contravention occurred.

2. Exit Not Blocked at Point of Entry

The critical question is whether the exit was clear at the moment the vehicle entered the box. Where CCTV evidence does not clearly establish this, the contravention is not made out.

3. CCTV Evidence

Code 31 is generally enforced by CCTV. The footage must clearly show the vehicle entering the box when the exit was not clear. Ambiguous or incomplete footage does not establish the contravention.

4. Yellow Box Marking Compliance

Box junction markings must comply with Diagram 1043 of TSRGD 2016. Any material non-compliance may render the restriction unenforceable.

6. Legal Framework

In Wales, moving traffic enforcement is governed by the Traffic Management Act 2004 and the Civil Enforcement of Parking Contraventions (Wales) Regulations 2008. The authority must demonstrate it holds the requisite powers and that all statutory requirements are met.

In light of the above, I respectfully request that the Penalty Charge Notice is cancelled in full.

If you do not accept these representations, please treat this letter as a formal request for the following evidence:

  • All Civil Enforcement Officer (CEO) notes and observations made at the time of the alleged contravention;
  • All photographic evidence taken by the CEO or any CCTV/camera system;
  • The relevant Traffic Regulation Order(s) (TRO) and any amendments or variations;
  • Confirmation that the TRO is valid, properly made and currently in force;
  • A full explanation of how all statutory requirements under the Traffic Management Act 2004 and the Civil Enforcement of Parking Contraventions (Wales) Regulations 2008 have been satisfied.
